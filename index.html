Welcome to Olympic Ski Resort! Here you will find that we have the best location, enriched with enticing history in Greek and Roman mythology. There is snow here year long, some say it is a gift from the gods, with dozens of trails to provide hours of enjoyment for skiers of all skill levels.

For those who wish to stay here longer, you may rent one of the wonderful rooms at our resort hotel. If you want to stay up to date on all your Olympus Skiing news, make sure to sign up for our newsletter located on our Contacts Page!
